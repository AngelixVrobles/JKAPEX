01 Company Overview

JK APEX Supply Group Inc is a Florida-based supplier specializing in office, janitorial, facility maintenance, safety, and breakroom products for commercial businesses and government agencies. Our mission is to provide quality products, competitive pricing, dependable service, and on-time delivery — while building long-term relationships with the customers and agencies we serve.

02 Core Competencies

  • Janitorial & Cleaning Supplies
  • Facility Maintenance Supplies
  • Safety Products & PPE
  • Breakroom & Food Service
  • Paper Products
  • Ink & Toner
  • Packaging & Shipping Supplies
  • Industrial & MRO Supplies
  • Furniture & Workspace Essentials

03 Differentiators

  • Competitive Pricing. Volume-backed pricing and transparent quotes that hold up against national suppliers.
  • Reliable Suppliers. A vetted supplier network that keeps the products you depend on in stock.
  • Fast Order Processing. Quotes returned quickly and orders moving the same business day.
  • Nationwide Delivery. Dependable, on-time fulfillment to commercial and government sites across the U.S.
  • Procurement-Focused. Structured to support agency solicitations, purchase cards, and compliant ordering.
  • Professional Service. A responsive team and a single, accountable point of contact for your account.
